The Best Movies According to Wikiedia

Top 10 movies of all-time.

  1. The Shawshank Redemption

    2. A man's journey through the prison system, emphasizing hope, friendship, and resilience.

  2. The Godfather

    3. A powerful crime family's patriarch navigating the world of organized crime and family loyalty.

  3. The Dark Knight

    4. Batman faces the Joker's chaos as he questions his role and the thin line between hero and villain.

  4. Schindler's List

    5. The true story of Oskar Schindler, a German businessman who saves over a thousand Polish Jews during the Holocaust.

  5. Pulp Fiction

    6. Interwoven stories of crime, redemption, and unexpected connections in the criminal underworld.

  6. Forrest Gump

    7. A man with a low IQ experiences pivotal moments in American history, proving that simplicity can lead to extraordinary outcomes.

  7. Inception

    8. A skilled thief enters people's dreams to steal their deepest secrets in this mind-bending exploration of reality and perception.

  8. The Matrix

    9. A computer hacker discovers the truth about the world—a simulated reality controlled by machines—and joins a rebellion.

  9. Titanic

    10. A love story set against the backdrop of the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic.

  10. Gladiator

    11. A betrayed Roman general seeks justice and revenge in ancient Rome's gladiatorial arenas.

  • These movies cover a range of genres and themes, from crime and drama to science fiction and historical epic.
